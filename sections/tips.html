# TIP NOTES
01 Money is key Save money before going you will never know how much you will spend, mabudol talaga kayo ng Japan Goodies
02 Get an IC Card at the Airport Suica or ICOCA. Load it up immediately. Works on trains, buses, convenience stores, vending machines. Don't wait until you're in the city.
03 Convenience Stores Are Actual Meals 7-Eleven, Lawson, FamilyMart. Legitimately good food. Onigiri, sandwiches, hot items. Also the easiest place to get cash with a foreign ATM card.
04 Google Maps Transit Mode is Perfect It knows every line, platform number, exit to use. Tokyo's train system looks intimidating but the app handles everything. Just trust it.
05 Always bring you Passport Always bring your passport to avail tax free shopping.
