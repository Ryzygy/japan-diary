Food
Faves

01
Gyukatsu Motomura
Crispy on the outside, juicy and tender on the inside. A premium beef cutlet that you finish cooking on a hot stone grill for your perfect doneness. Simple, interactive, and incredibly satisfying.
02
Tonkatsu Oribe
If you’re a bit far from Ueno, this spot is definitely worth the trip. Their pork cutlet is incredibly juicy and perfectly crispy, literally the kind of flavor that makes you want to come back again and again. After my first bite, I ended up eating here multiple times. It’s that good.
03
Ichiran
Ichiran Super satisfying and definitely worth it for under ¥2,000. The broth is rich, flavorful, and comforting. It is the kind of ramen that makes you realize what authentic ramen is really supposed to taste like. After trying this, you will understand the hype. It is filling, deeply satisfying, and on a completely different level.
04
7/11 Konbini
If you don’t have time, this is the place you’ll naturally end up going to. There are so many meals to choose from, and everything is fresh, not old stock. It is quick, convenient, and surprisingly satisfying. I highly recommend their egg sandwich. It is simple but seriously so good.
