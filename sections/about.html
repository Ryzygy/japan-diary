Japan has always been on the list. Then one day, it was not just on the list anymore. It was happening. This is a personal record of everything seen, eaten, and felt during those eight days.

I did this trip completely on my own, a true solo adventure from January 27 to February 3. Eight days based entirely in Tokyo, with a day trip to Echigo Yuzawa and a final stretch in Asakusa before flying home from Narita. No grand plan, just the city, my Suica card and cash, and a serious willingness to walk until my feet gave out.

After this experience, I honestly feel like I do not need to try other countries. I will always be coming back here. Japan feels like a place I will never get tired of.
